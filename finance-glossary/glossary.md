## Beta
A measure of a stock's volatility compared to the overall market; beta > 1 means more volatile.

## Dividend
A payout to shareholders from the company's profits.

## Earnings Per Share
A company's net profit divided by the number of outstanding shares.

## MACD
Moving Average Convergence Divergence: a trend-following momentum indicator.

## Market Capitalization
Total value of a company's outstanding shares; calculated as share price Ã— number of shares.

## Moving Average
An average of stock prices over a period to smooth out short-term fluctuations.

## P/E Ratio
Price-to-Earnings ratio: stock price divided by earnings per share.

## RSI
Relative Strength Index: a momentum indicator measuring the magnitude of recent price changes.

## Volatility
Statistical measure of the dispersion of returns; often measured using standard deviation.

## Volume
The number of shares traded during a specific period.
